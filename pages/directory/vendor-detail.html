Our Story

Born from a passion for traditional French pastry techniques combined with locally sourced Indonesian ingredients. At Bite & Bliss Bakery, we believe that the best bread starts with simple, high-quality components and the luxury of time. Every croissant is hand-laminated over 72 hours to ensure the perfect honeycomb structure and buttery crunch that our campus community has come to love.
